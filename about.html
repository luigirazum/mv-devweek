DevWeek Logo
How was DW Logo chosen

The logo of DevWeek was decided through the logo competition from 10.Jan to 10.Feb.

After online public voting and Selection Committee's judging process, the logo created by Justin Leitner won the prize.

After consultation with the Selection Committee we optimized the prize-winning-logo, so it could be the Oficial DW Logo for the rest of the year or until another public competition is open.

Please do not edit, change, distort, recolor, or reconfigure the DW Logo.
